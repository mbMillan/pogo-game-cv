Pogo Bounce Mechanic

A movement system built around constant bouncing. The player doesn't fully "jump" in a traditional way; instead, movement is always part of a bounce cycle that you influence rather than directly control.

Momentum-based movement

Movement keeps its momentum, direction changes are dependent on your speed and require thinking ahead of time.

Sprint

Holding the sprint button increases movement speed and bounce frequency, but makes controls tighter and less forgiving.

The Stomp Mechanic

Pressing the jump button triggers a ground stomp that will boost the next bounce when you hit the ground.

Breakable platforms

Some platforms can only take a limited number of hits before breaking. In this case, the block breaks after three impacts.

Slopes and bounce direction

Bounce direction follows the surface normal, so slopes and uneven ground affect movement. There's also a max slope angle for what counts as "walkable".
