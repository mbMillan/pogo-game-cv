Pogo Bounce Mechanic

A custom movement component specifically made for a constantly-bouncing pogo stick.
The original idea was about a platformer game where you don't have absolute control over the jump mechanic,

Momentum-based movement

The whole movement system is momentum-based.
Direction changes are slow and require thinking ahead of time.

Sprint

By holding down the Sprint button, the character starts sprinting.
Movement speed and bounce frequency get faster while controls get tighter.

The Stomp Mechanic

By pressing the (would be) jump button the character performs a stomp.
This will force a stronger bounce as soon as the pogo touches the ground.

Unstable platforms

Platforms that can only sustain a limited amount of force before crumbling.
In the example, the block falls (through the ground) after the player touches it three times.

Bounce direction and slopes

The bounce direction takes into consideration the normal vector of the ground surface,
this allows for the use of sloped and irregular ground for gameplay purposes and it lets me set a maximum "walkable" slope, when needed.
